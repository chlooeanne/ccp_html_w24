Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich

What you'll need:

  • Two slices of bread
  • A spoonful of peanut butter
  • A spoonful of jelly

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches are a simple and quick meal.

How to assemble:

  1. Take your two slices of bread and lay them flat on a plate.
  2. Spread the spoonful of peanut butter one the inside of one of the slices of bread.
  3. Spread the spoonful of jelly on the inside of the other slice of bread.
  4. Combine the two slices of bread, connecting the peanut butter to the jelly.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
